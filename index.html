About Me

Born in Brazil, I now call Italy home. I've been a footballer since I was young, but my love for problem-solving and creating interesting visuals led me to pursue programming and design. My journey began in 2023 when I discovered Photoshop. I've been using it to create eye-catching matchday flyers for professional athletes, infusing my designs with creativity and a touch of flair.

As a software engineering student, I'm constantly learning and exploring the latest technologies. I'm fascinated by the potential of coding to solve problems and create innovative solutions.

I find joy in exploring new ideas, pushing boundaries, and using my skills to bring my visions to life. I'm excited to see what I can learn and I am eager to continue improving, growing, and making a positive impact in both my professional and personal life.
